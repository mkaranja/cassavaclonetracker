Receiving New Planting Materials:

From the left panel: Do the following, 

  • Enter the receiving date. Today's date is automatically selected (required).
  • Select the variety name from the drop down list (required). 
  • Select the source of planting material from the drop down list (required). 

In the main panel, 

  • Enter the number of mini-cuttings (required).
  • Click the Button 'Assign Barcode' to scan and assign pre-generated barcode label to the box. Thes scanned barcode now becomes the box identity and should be placed on the box with mini-cuttings. 
  • If you have many mini-cuttings, repeat step 1 and 2 to assign them to more boxes.

NB: Ensure that the selected variety is same as one in the scanned barcode. 

Once done entering the data for the new mini-cuttings, click 'Submit' to save to the database.
